Athena's Web Weekly Column

  Week of January 30th - February 5th,  1991

Crossroads

Gulf War Archive

   There is a choice that looms on the horizon before us,

US Flag
and our future will be determined by it. Will the United States forge a new path through the wilderness, hewing out the beginnings of a new world order for future generations to follow? Or will she miss her mark and plunge into the void; a dark and dangerous direction where the shadows grow long and the dim light is hidden to all but the most adept?

George Bush
   The choice is this: If the U.S. can hold off until the ides March before beginning their land war, then the cost, particularly in human lives, will be greatly minimized. If, on the other hand, the U.S. begins their land war before the 15th of March, then we are looking at potential disaster in the Persian Gulf.

   It's as simple as that. In mid-March, the U.S. progressed Moon will enter a new sign, Capricorn, and as a nation we will begin to experience a new emotional vitality. Any endeavors looking for public support will be particularly affected.

Hussein
If, on the other hand, the U.S. begins its ground war before mid-March, then we are looking at an entirely different picture. Currently, the U.S. progressed Moon is Void of Course in Sagittarius. In effect, this is a waning, dead energy. Seeds planted on this time will die on the vine.

   Actually, the air war segment of this conflict did start during this Void of Course Moon period, and if your rooting for the US/UN, that's not good. It can be done, but it will be costly. In addition, two major planetary indicators, the U.S. progressed MC squaring Pluto on Feb. 26th, and the Full Moon on the 28th, indicate that events are coming to a boil. A corresponding alignment in Iraq's chart earmarks March 6th.

   Since early last September we've been drawing attention to these late February-early March dates. Now the news media and the military are starting to focus on them, too.

At these times, a crossroads of decision will be reached. Celestial advice would have put us on a far different track at a much earlier date, but given the current scenario and with the decisions before us, there are still choices available as to how to best deal with upcoming hurdles.

   How can we individually face these choices? By realizing that this is the beginning of the awakening.
