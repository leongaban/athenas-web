  Week of September 10th - September 16th,  1990

Iraq Attack

Gulf War Archive

   There are a couple of flash points coming up in Iraq's natal chart.

US Flag
At their best they indicate that Saddam Hussein is going to be his general bellicose self, inciting those that follow him to pitch that hasn't been seen in a while. There are no questions that events are going to come to a head at these times. Free will is still available for those to choose as they will as to how to react to these circumstances, but the ingredients in this brew are powerful indeed. Should we successfully navigate the dangerous waters that are ahead, a real catastrophe may be averted.

   First the dates. On Sept. 22nd, 1990 Mars will be parallel to Iraq's natal Ascendant by progression. Then on Nov. 8th, this same Mars will square Iraq's natal Mercury. On its own, this later aspect could deal with the embargo coming to a head. Mercury is the planet that rules merchants and trade, and this is the time that circumstances will be most difficult for them.

Vocal accusation and assaults are also a principal manifestation of this combination. Belligerent declarations are more self-serving (and self-destructive), than at other times. But of the two aspects this one frightens me less. It is Mars paralleling the Ascendant that bears more of the brunt of celestial concern. If attack is going to come; either against Iraq or by them, this will be the underlying energy supplying it.

   On the upside, the transiting planets which triggered both the attack on Iran in 1980 and Kuwait in August are not present exactly on the 22nd, but there are some aspects on the 18th that could ignite the whole powder keg. So, we are looking at the period between the 17th and 22nd of this month to determine just how these events are going to be handled. Since Iraq's Ascendant is a water sign, it's possible that we are looking here at a naval confrontation, which would presumably be more limited than anything that might transpire on land,

but unfortunately, since Cancer (Iraq's Ascendant) is also vitally concerned with the national interest, this is not the only way that it could come through.

   There you have it. Watch the periods around the 22nd of September and the 8th of November. For those who have a mind to, collective prayers and meditation would be helpful.
