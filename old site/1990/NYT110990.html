Advocate Newspapers, 10 September, 1990

   "There are a couple of flash points coming up in Iraq's natal chart. At their best they indicate that Saddam Hussein is going to be his general bellicose self, inciting those that follow him to pitch that hasn't been seen in a while. There are no questions that events are going to come to a head at these times."

US Flag

   "First the dates. On Sept. 22nd, 1990 Mars will be parallel to Iraq's natal Ascendant by progression. Then on Nov. 8th, this same Mars will square Iraq's natal Mercury. On its own, this later aspect could deal with the embargo coming to a head. Mercury is the planet that rules merchants and trade, and this is the time that circumstances will be most difficult for them. Vocal accusation and assaults are also a principal manifestation of this combination. Belligerent declarations are more self-serving (and self-destructive), than at other times. "

   "There you have it. Watch the periods around the 22nd of September and the 8th of November. "

The New York Times

Friday, November 9, 1990
Page One

BUSH SENDS NEW UNITS TO GULF
TO PROVIDE 'OFFENSIVE OPTION;
U.S. FORCE COULD REACH 380,000
TANK BUILDUP A KEY
President Suggests New Troops
May Persuade Iraq to Back Down

   WASHINGTON, Nov. 8 - President Bush today ordered more than 150,000 additional

George Bush
American ground, sea and air forces to the Persian Gulf area saying they were needed to provide "an adequate offensive military option" to drive Iraqi troops from Kuwait.

   The move would nearly double the size of the American land and naval combat units in the area and give the United States a numerical advantage over Iraq in the most up-to-date tanks.

   In making his announcement, Mr. Bush said the reinforcements were intended to increase pressure on President Saddam Hussein of Iraq to withdraw his troops from Kuwait and to lay the foundation for an American attack should he continue to refuse.
